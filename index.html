About Cathie Wood
Cathie Wood is a renowned American investment manager, currently serving as founder and CEO of ARK Investment Management. Ms. Wood has extensive experience in investment management, and her founded ARK Investment focuses on researching investment opportunities in innovative technology sectors. The company manages multiple actively managed ETF products, including the ARK Innovation ETF (ARKK), primarily investing in technology-related industries. She has become a focus of industry attention and earned the reputation as the "female Warren Buffett" or "tech stock goddess."
